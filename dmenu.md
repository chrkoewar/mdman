## keys
- `C-Y`         paste from clipboard
- `C-y`         paste from selection

- `Ctrl+w`      delete the word on the left
- `Alt+b`       move cursor to the start of the current word
- `Ctrl-Left`   ...
- `Alt+f`       move cursor to the end of the current word
- `Ctrl-Right`   ...
